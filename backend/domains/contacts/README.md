# Contacts Domain

The contacts domain is NoblePort's master CRM directory — every person the
business has ever spoken to, regardless of whether they ever became a lead,
client, or subcontractor. It exists once and is referenced from leads, jobs,
follow-ups, and marketing attribution so we never duplicate humans.

Each contact carries its full interaction history (calls, emails, site visits,
notes) and can be linked to other contacts (spouses, business partners,
referrers) so we understand the social graph that drives word-of-mouth in the
custom-build market.

## Capabilities

- Create, update, and search contacts
- Find-or-create by email/phone to deduplicate inbound traffic
- Merge duplicate contact records (preserving history on the primary)
- Log every interaction (email, call, SMS, meeting, site visit, note)
- Pull a complete contact history view (all interactions in order)
- Capture do-not-contact status with a reason
- Define inter-contact relationships (spouse, partner, referrer, etc.)

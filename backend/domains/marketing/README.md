# Marketing Domain

The marketing domain owns campaigns, their creative assets, the spend we
attribute to them, and the lead-attribution touch points that connect ad
dollars to closed jobs. It is the source of truth for "where do our deals come
from?" reporting.

Channels span paid (Google Ads, Facebook Ads), organic (SEO, blog), outbound
(email, direct mail), and offline (events, referral programs). Each lead can
collect multiple attribution touches (first, last, assist) so we can attribute
revenue across a multi-touch journey.

## Capabilities

- Create and manage campaigns with status, budget, dates, and UTM defaults
- Catalog marketing assets (ads, landing pages, email templates, flyers) per campaign
- Record additional spend against a campaign as it runs
- Attribute inbound leads to campaigns via UTM parameters
- Compute ROI per campaign (revenue / spend) from won estimates
- Roll up channel performance across a date range
- Summarize multi-touch attribution across the full lead set

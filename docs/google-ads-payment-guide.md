# Google Ads Payment Guide (iPhone)

Step-by-step instructions to log in and pay your Google Ads balance safely from an iPhone.

---

## Step 1: Open Google Ads Directly

Do **not** use any email link. Open Safari and navigate to:

```
ads.google.com
```

Sign in with the Google account tied to the Ads account.

---

## Step 2: Switch to the Correct Account (if needed)

1. Tap your **profile icon** (top right).
2. Confirm you are in the correct Google account.
3. If you manage multiple accounts, tap the **account selector** and choose the correct Ads ID.

---

## Step 3: Go to Billing

1. Tap the **three-line menu** in the top left.
2. Tap **Billing**.
3. Tap **Billing & payments**.

---

## Step 4: Pay the Balance

1. Tap **Summary**.
2. Tap **Make a payment**.
3. Pay the **full outstanding balance** (accounts usually won't reactivate until fully paid).

---

## Step 5: Update Payment Method (if needed)

1. In Billing, tap **Payment methods**.
2. Add or update card / bank info.
3. Save, then go back and make the payment.

---

## Safety Checklist

- **Never** pay from a link in an email.
- Only pay inside `ads.google.com`.
- Confirm status under **Billing > Transactions**.
- If anything looks unusual, stop and verify the account details before proceeding.
